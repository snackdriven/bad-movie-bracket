# Worst Movie Tournament

You are going to sit down, open this bracket, and be forced to decide whether *Cats* is worse than *Catwoman*. In writing. With your name on it.

There is no good answer. There is only the answer you can live with.

**[Enter at your own risk ‚Üí](https://snackdriven.github.io/bad-movie-bracket/)**

## What is this

32 movies. One question, repeated 31 times: which of these is worse.

Not bad in a boring way. Bad in a *Tommy Wiseau spent $6 million importing Korean leather jackets* way. Bad in a *Joel Schumacher apologized for the Batsuit nipples separately from apologizing for the whole movie* way. These films were made by real humans with real budgets and at no point did anyone in the room say "wait."

Pick the worst in each matchup. Keep going until one film stands alone as the undisputed champion of failure. It will feel like a victory for it. It shouldn't.

Every single one earned their spot. Some of them fought very hard for it.

If you think something belongs here and isn't, open an issue. I'm not actively expanding the bracket but I do want to know what you think got robbed.

## Features

- Notes on every movie, because you are going to need to justify yourself later
- Trivia after every pick (The Fanatic made $3,153 opening weekend. Three thousand dollars. That is not a formatting error.)
- Upset tracking when a lower seed beats a higher one, because yes, rankings exist, and yes, they can be wrong
- Undo, because you will change your mind about Cats vs. Catwoman at least once
- Full bracket view so you can see the carnage spread across all five rounds
- Cloud sync so this can follow you across devices like a bad dream
- Film grain overlay because honestly it felt right
